Go To Thud Homepage

Thud is a graphical 'heads up display' for playing BattleTech MUXes (for example, 3030MUX or Frontiers). It features a tactical map, contacts list, and more. Thud is designed for people new to BTech MUX and veterans alike.


Go To Thump Homepage

Thump is a graphical map editor based on the Thud code. It features hex-by-hex undo, elevation and terrain painting, and more. Thump is designed for people who want to create maps for any BTech MUX.




Both Thud and Thump are open-source, and distributed under the BSD license. They are hosted by Sourceforge.net.
